Our Mission

Huq Engineering Consultancy, PLLC is a consulting engineering firm providing services of Geotechnical Engineering, Structural Engineering, Forensic Engineering, Construction support, and Special Inspection. We also provide services of building planning, design, permitting for new buildings and alterations to existing buildings. We provide innovative solutions to our projects. Our business is founded on the principles of providing high quality service in a timely and cost effective manner.

About Us

Huq Engineering Consultancy is a qualified consulting firm with the following certifications:

  1. Professional Engineer in the State of New York, registered by the NYC Department of Buildings
  2. Professional Engineer in the State of Maryland and Texas
  3. Special Inspection Agency, certified by the NYC Department of Buildings
  4. Minority Business Enterprise certified by the State of New York
  5. Minority Business Enterprise certified by New York City
  6. Minority Business Enterprise certified by the Port Authority of New York and New Jersey
